# 1. SYSTEM PURPOSE

TOS_4_IPE (Interview Performance Engine) is the canonical interview execution module of TOS.

Its purpose is to:

- convert positioning into interview performance,
- maximize conversion probability,
- prepare strategic answers,
- strengthen executive presence,
- defend credibility,
- manage objections,
- control compensation narratives,
- and optimize final hiring outcomes.

This module transforms strategic positioning into real-world conversion.

No major interview should proceed without IPE preparation.
